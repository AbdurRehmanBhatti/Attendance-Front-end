# Privacy Policy for TimeSphere

Effective date: April 4, 2026
Last updated: April 4, 2026

TimeSphere (the App) is an employee attendance application used to clock in, clock out, and view attendance history. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

By using the App, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Who Controls Your Data

For workplace attendance use, your employer or organization is generally the data controller for attendance and account records. The App provider processes data to deliver app features on behalf of the organization.

## 2. Information We Collect

The App may collect and process the following categories of data:

1. Account and identity data
- Work email or username used to sign in.
- Profile data provided by your organization's backend, such as user ID, display name, company ID, and role.

2. Attendance data
- Clock-in and clock-out records.
- Attendance history and summary data.

3. Location data (during clock actions)
- Precise and/or approximate device location when you clock in or clock out.
- Location is used to support attendance verification and work-location context.

4. Authentication and session data
- Access token and session metadata.
- Refresh token used to keep your session active.

5. Technical and network data
- Basic request and response metadata required to connect the App with backend services.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

- Authenticate users and manage secure sessions.
- Record attendance events.
- Display attendance history and summaries.
- Support password reset and account security flows.
- Maintain service reliability, performance, and security.

## 4. Location Permission and Usage

The App requests location permission to support attendance clock actions.

- Location is collected at clock-in or clock-out time (or the most recent available reading if a real-time fix is unavailable).
- The App is not designed for continuous background location tracking for attendance.
- If location permission is denied, attendance functionality may be limited based on organizational policy.

## 5. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information.

Data may be shared only as needed with:

- Your organization's attendance backend and APIs to provide core app features.
- Service or infrastructure providers acting on behalf of your organization.
- Legal, regulatory, or law-enforcement authorities when required by applicable law.

## 6. Data Retention

- Attendance and account data retention is controlled by your organization and its backend systems.
- Session data stored on your device is retained only as long as needed to keep you signed in and is removed when you sign out or the session is cleared.

## 7. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including:

- Secure local storage for sensitive session data (for example, refresh tokens).
- Protected storage for non-sensitive session metadata.
- Authenticated API requests to protected endpoints.

No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, but we take appropriate steps to protect your data.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- Access or correct your personal information.
- Request deletion of personal information.
- Restrict or object to certain processing.

To exercise rights, contact your organization's administrator or the privacy contact below.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for workplace attendance use and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on where your organization or service providers host systems.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by updating the Last updated date and, where required, by additional notice.
